### mac上jenkins记录安装
- brew install jenkins
- which  jenkins 查看安装位置
- jenkins 启动
- jenkins -v 版本查看



### url上的开关命令

```
http://localhost:8080/exit //退出Jenkins
http://localhost:8080/restart //重启
http://localhost:8080/reload //重新加载
```
